Welcome to the Texoma Weekend Roundup.

This guide brings together events, live music, family activities,
local businesses, lake-day ideas, and community happenings from
across the Texoma region.

## Thursday

Check local venue pages for Thursday-night live music, trivia,
food specials, and community activities.

## Friday

Friday night is a strong time for live music, karaoke,
community gatherings, restaurants, and local entertainment
around Texoma.

## Saturday

Saturday typically offers the widest variety of markets,
family events, benefit activities, live performances,
lake recreation, and nightlife.

## Sunday

Wrap up the weekend with family outings, sports watch parties,
local restaurants, parks, and community events.

## Submit an event

Organizers may email event information and flyers to:

**texomaweekendguide@gmail.com**

Please include the date, time, venue, city, description,
official link, and promotional image.
