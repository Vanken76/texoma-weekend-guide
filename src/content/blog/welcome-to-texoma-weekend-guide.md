Texoma Weekend Guide was created to help people discover more
of what is happening throughout North Texas and southern Oklahoma.

The region is full of live music, family events, restaurants,
community fundraisers, lake activities, small businesses,
markets, sports, and places worth visiting.

The challenge is that the information is often scattered across
dozens of pages, groups, flyers, and event listings.

TWG brings those discoveries together.

## What you will find here

- Weekend Roundups
- Upcoming events
- Live music
- Family activities
- Food and local businesses
- Lake Texoma recreation
- Community news and highlights
- Local guides and recommendations

## The coverage area

TWG may cover Denison, Sherman, Pottsboro, Gainesville,
Whitesboro, Bonham, Durant, Tishomingo, Lake Texoma,
and nearby communities.

This is only the beginning.

**Discover Local. Make Memories.**
